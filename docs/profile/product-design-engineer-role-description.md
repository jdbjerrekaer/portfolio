# Product Design Engineer role description

The Product Design Engineer is a hybrid role bridging product strategy, user experience design, and engineering feasibility. The role owns the end-to-end product design process from research and concept through high-fidelity design, prototyping, implementation partnership, and product quality in production.

## Core responsibilities

**Product design:** End-to-end design of product features and flows across the platforms, covering interaction design, visual design, prototyping, and accessibility. Ensures designs are understandable for software developers and aligned with established design principles.

**Research and customer insight:** Product UX research and customer feedback synthesis, including ethnographic studies, translated into prioritized UX improvements.

**Design-to-code quality:** Partners with software developers throughout implementation, providing specifications, design input, and testing while features are built.

**Product strategy partnership:** Partners with Product Management on requirements, goals, and roadmaps. Acts as the primary product design point of contact for platform development throughout the development lifecycle.

## Ownership

The Product Design Engineer holds primary ownership of the design system and motion system, contributes to product quality in production, and works closely with software developers on the shared React component library, UI Toolkit.

**Design system:** Primary maintainer and gatekeeper of the design system: components, styles, variables, and documentation. Ensures contributions meet quality, accessibility, and technical feasibility standards.

**Product quality:** Leads design and feature QA across the product surface: verifies that requested features are implemented and working, that shipped product matches intended design and behavior, and that accessibility and usability hold up from a design perspective.

**Stakeholder collaboration:** Brand and visual identity direction is provided by the design manager. Marketing collaborates on brand-related visuals and product/marketing consistency. Engineering provides feedback on technical feasibility and implementation standards.
