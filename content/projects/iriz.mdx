## Overview

Iriz is a specialized port of our desktop and mobile partner platform for affiliate marketing, designed exclusively for dynamic CPC (Cost Per Click) pricing. The platform enables influencers to connect with exclusive brands through a streamlined, performance-based affiliate marketing system.
